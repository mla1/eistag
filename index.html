About

Eistag
noun, /aistaÀêk/
Literally ice day, more meaningfully translated to ice cream day.
Eistag is a holiday observed mainly in Vienna, Austria. Traditionally, it is celebrated by eating ice cream, either by itself or in combination with other sweets, for example apple pie or Austrian pan cakes, called Palatschinken.
